New class for Fall 2015

Date Wed 08 April 2015

This site is the tentative homepage for a new course at UC Berkeley titled Reproducible and Collaborative Statistical Data Science. This is a 4-unit course offered by the Department of Statistics for the first time in Fall 2015.

The course is based on the Stat 157 that was offered in Fall 2013 and Fall 2014.

In Fall 2015, the class will be offered jointly to undergraduates (Stat 159) and graduate students (Stat 259). Graduate standing is required to register for Stat 259.

Stat 133, 134, and 135 are prerequisites.
